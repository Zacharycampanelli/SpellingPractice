Spelling Practice

Rules of the Game

In this game, you will hear a word by clicking the text bubble. Enter the word (spelled correctly!) into the input box and press submit. Your goal is to spell as many words correctly as you can within 60 seconds. Good luck!

Please enter your name, then press "ENTER"

When you are finished, press "CONTINUE"
